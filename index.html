About This Site

If you're on this page, you're probably wondering what this site is all about. A few students at GVSU wanted to create a central hub to talk about all of the great things that students can do both on and off campus. This is the result!

Who is this site for?

This site was created for current and future students, but any person with interest in GVSU and the GVSU community can benefit from this site! We aim to advertise all of the things that make GVSU a great place to be.

Is this site ran by GVSU?

This site is NOT ran by GVSU. The students that manage this site do so independently.

Can students submit content to this site?

Absolutely! Students can submit content to this site by filling out the form below!
